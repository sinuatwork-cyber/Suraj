Urja Mitra Partner Role

As an Urja Mitra Partner, you are the face of clean energy in your local community. You bridge the gap between rural/semi-urban households and modern solar solutions.

assignment Core Responsibilities

  • Conduct initial rooftop feasibility checks via app.
  • Educate customers on subsidy schemes and ROI.
  • Collect primary documents for lead verification.

how_to_reg Requirements

  • Valid Aadhar Card & PAN Card.
  • Basic understanding of smartphone usage.
  • Residence in target district (UP only).

Installation Partner Role

As an EPC partner, you manage the technical execution. We provide the verified leads and the inventory; you provide the engineering expertise and manpower.

engineering Technical Workflow

  • Detailed 3D site survey and shadow analysis.
  • Custom BOQ (Bill of Quantities) upload for approval.
  • Digital milestone tracking for payment release.

business Requirements

  • Registered GST business entity.
  • Minimum 2 certified solar technicians.
  • Previous solar installation track record (min 10kW).

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started.

What is the onboarding process for new partners? expand_more
The process involves application submission, document verification (Aadhar/PAN/GST), a 2-day virtual training workshop, and final certification before you start receiving leads.
When and how are payments released? expand_more
Urja Mitra agents receive weekly payouts for every verified lead. Installation partners receive milestone-based payments: 20% on site survey, 50% on BOQ delivery, and 30% on commissioning.
Do I need to carry my own equipment for surveys? expand_more
Agents only need a smartphone with our app. Installation partners are expected to have basic measurement tools and safety gear. Specialized digital survey tools are provided by Urja Mitra.
Is there an exclusive territory for partners? expand_more
We assign districts to partners based on capacity. While there's no strict exclusivity, we manage lead distribution to ensure every partner has a healthy project volume.
